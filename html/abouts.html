My college life

I started university in 2019 ,I was 17(almost 18) years old.To be honest I was scared of getting in and fitting in the first time but I got over the anxiety pretty well.I found new friends that really fits my personality and we spend good times together.

I strugged managing my time during the first weeks of starting learning as it was way different than highschool.But then i started using my time well by going into library and everything just seemed to fit well.

Going back to the courses,the first semester was mostly things we already learnt in highschool but some of them are new.

After finishing the first semester,I was going to start my second semester but the COVID case hit and classes were delayed for a year,a whole year!but after a year of sitting in home doing nothing we got bacl again.But it was so messy,1 semester was like 2 month and we were rushing the courses a lot.

The semester being this short really affected my grade,I wasn't able to keep up with what we are learning,there was a lot assignments ans exams that made our life so much worse.Till this semester,we were rushing like each semester was 2-3 month top but this year we are caught up to the time we wasted in our house and now I can finally calmly breath peacefully.
